Purpose of the Beta Test Program

The KindredCircl Beta Program is designed to gather feedback on the KindredCircl platform prior to its official launch, to identify potential issues, and to improve the user experience.

Beta Tester Responsibilities

Use the Platform:

Beta Testers agree to actively use the KindredCircl platform and its features.

Provide Feedback:

Beta Testers agree to provide regular and constructive feedback on their experience, including but not limited to any bugs, errors, usability issues, and suggestions for improvement.

Adhere to Guidelines:

Beta Testers agree to adhere to the KindredCircl Community Guidelines (as outlined in document version 2025.01, or any subsequent updated version), which promote:

  • Genuine Connection
  • Authentic Expression
  • Mutual Respect
  • Inclusive Belonging
  • Respectful Expression

Confidentiality:

Beta Testers agree to keep confidential any non-public information about KindredCircl, including but not limited to features, functionality, design, and performance.

Prompt Reporting:

Beta Testers agree to promptly report any bugs, errors, or security vulnerabilities they encounter.

KindredCircl's Rights and Responsibilities

Provide Access to the Platform

KindredCircl will provide Beta Testers with access to the beta platform.

Collect Feedback from Beta Testers

KindredCircl will collect and review feedback provided by Beta Testers.

Modify the Platform

KindredCircl reserves the right to modify or discontinue the beta program or any features of the beta platform at any time.

Use Feedback

KindredCircl may use Beta Tester feedback for any purpose, including but not limited to improving the platform and promoting KindredCircl to potential users. Beta Testers agree that KindredCircl may use their feedback in promotional materials, such as testimonials or case studies. In such cases, only the Beta Tester's first name and last initial will be used.

Code of Conduct

Respectful Interaction:

Beta Testers agree to interact with KindredCircl staff, other beta testers, and community members in a respectful and constructive manner.

Prohibited Conduct:

Beta Testers agree to refrain from any prohibited conduct as outlined in the KindredCircl Community Guidelines, including but not limited to:

  • Hate Speech and Discrimination
  • Harassment and Bullying
  • Violence and Threats
  • Spam and Misinformation

Privacy

Data Collection

KindredCircl will collect and use Beta Testers' personal information in accordance with its Privacy Policy.

Data Usage

Beta Testers consent to the use of their feedback and activity data to improve the KindredCircl platform.

Disclaimer of Warranty

• The KindredCircl beta platform is provided "as is" and KindredCircl makes no warranties, express or implied, regarding its performance or functionality.

Limitation of Liability

KindredCircl shall not be liable for any damages arising out of or related to the Beta Tester's participation in the Beta Program.

Termination

KindredCircl may terminate a Beta Tester's participation in the Beta Program at any time for any reason.

Beta Testers may terminate their participation at any time by notifying KindredCircl.
