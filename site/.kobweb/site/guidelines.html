Prohibited Content and Behaviors

Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)

The creation, distribution, or possession of Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate account termination and reporting to law enforcement.

This prohibition applies to all forms of CSAM, including but not limited to:

  • Real or photographic CSAM.
  • Computer-generated or digitally manipulated CSAM.
  • Drawn, painted, or otherwise artistically rendered CSAM.

Hate Speech

KindredCircl does not tolerate hate speech. Do not post content that attacks or demeans people based on race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics.

Harassment and Bullying

Harassment, intimidation, or bullying of any kind is prohibited. This includes targeted insults, threats, or attempts to silence others.

Violence and Threats

Content that glorifies violence, incites violence against individuals or groups, promotes self-harm (including live streaming of self-harm), or threatens harm to animals or property destruction is strictly prohibited.

Illegal Activities

Any content or activity that promotes or facilitates illegal activities, such as drug trafficking, fraud, identity theft, or provides instruction on how to undertake such activities, is prohibited.

Spam and Misinformation

We prohibit content that promotes or glorifies dangerous behavior, self-harm, illegal activity, or the use of weapons to threaten or harm others.

Impersonation and Misrepresentation

Malicious impersonation of another person, creating fake accounts with harmful intent, fraudulent misrepresentation of your identity, falsely claiming association with an organization to deceive others, or misrepresenting your professional qualifications for fraudulent purposes is prohibited.

This guideline does not prohibit individuals from expressing their gender identity, engaging in artistic performances (such as drag), or using pseudonyms, provided such actions are not intended to deceive, defraud, or harm others.

Intellectual Property Infringement:

General Principles

KindredCircl respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects its users to do the same.

Users are prohibited from posting or sharing content that infringes on the intellectual property rights of others, including copyrights, trademarks, and patents.

Intellectual property rights belong to the creator or owner.

Copyright Infringement

Copyright infringement occurs when you use copyrighted material without permission from the copyright owner. This includes, but is not limited to, copying, distributing, displaying, or creating derivative works of copyrighted material. This includes images, text, videos, music, and software.

Users are responsible for obtaining explicit permission from the copyright holder before using any copyrighted material.

Even when fair use applies, users should always credit the original creator and provide proper attribution.

Examples of use that are not fair use include but are not limited to, uploading entire movies or music albums.

For more information, read the DMCA Policy or email dmca@kindredcircl.com.

Trademark Infringement

Users are prohibited from using trademarks in a way that is likely to cause confusion about the source or affiliation of products or services.

Business profiles must accurately represent their brands and obtain necessary trademark rights.

Business profiles must provide accurate and complete information about their company, products, and services. They must not engage in deceptive or misleading practices.

Business profiles must have the right to use any branding that they use on their profile.

For more information, read the DMCA Policy or email dmca@kindredcircl.com.

Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA)

KindredCircl complies with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA). Our full DMCA policy can be found here.

To report DMCA violations, please email dmca@kindredcircl.com. Alternatively, you can submit reports using the forms available on the KindredCircl website and mobile application

Business profiles must provide accurate and complete information about their company, products, and services. They must not engage in deceptive or misleading practices.

Business profiles must have the right to use any branding that they use on their profile.

For more information, read the DMCA Policy or email dmca@kindredcircl.com.

Reporting Intellectual Property Infringement:

When reporting any type of intellectual property infringement, please include the following information:

  • Your contact information
  • A description of the copyrighted work, trademark, or patent
  • Proof of ownership
  • The location of the infringing material on KindredCircl
  • A statement of good faith belief that the use is not authorized
  • A statement, under penalty of perjury, that the information is accurate

KindredCircl will promptly remove or disable access to the infringing material upon receipt of a valid notice.

Users who believe their content was wrongly removed may submit a counter-notice. KindredCircl reserves the right to terminate the accounts of repeat infringers as outlined in the DMCA Policy.

Self-Harm and Mental Health Support

At KindredCircl, we care deeply about the mental health and well-being of our community members. We understand that some individuals may struggle with self-harm or suicidal thoughts.

If you or someone you know is experiencing thoughts of self-harm or suicide, please know that you are not alone and help is available. We strongly encourage you to reach out to the following resources:

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988

Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741

The Trevor Project: 1-866-488-7386 (for LGBTQ youth)

We understand that sharing personal struggles can be difficult, and we encourage open and supportive conversations within our community. However, to protect the safety of all members, we have the following guidelines:

  • Content that glorifies or encourages self-harm or suicide is strictly prohibited and will be removed.
  • Live streaming of self-harm or suicide attempts will be immediately interrupted and removed.
  • In the event of a live-streamed self-harm or suicide attempt, KindredCircl will make every reasonable effort to:
    • Immediately preserve any information that could assist in locating the individual.
    • Contact emergency services or mental health crisis teams in the user's suspected location, if possible.
    • Provide any available contact information to emergency services to assist them in their efforts.
    • If the user is known to have Kin, KindredCircl will attempt to contact the kin to alert them of the situation.
  • If you encounter content that you believe promotes or encourages self-harm or suicide, or see a live stream of self-harm, please report it immediately to selfharm@kindredcircl.com. Alternatively, you can submit reports using the forms available on the KindredCircl website and mobile application..

We want KindredCircl to be a place of support. If you are struggling, please reach out to the resources above, or talk to someone you trust.

Accessibility Tools and Features

KindredCircl is committed to providing an inclusive experience for all users. We recognize that individuals with disabilities may utilize various accessibility tools and features to interact with our platform.

General Accessibility Features:

Web

  • Websites should adhere to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) to ensure accessibility.
  • Common web accessibility features include:
    • Screen reader compatibility (e.g., JAWS, NVDA)
    • Keyboard navigation
    • Text alternatives for images (alt text)
    • Sufficient color contrast
    • Resizable text
    • Semantic HTML structure
  • Users can utilize browser extensions and accessibility tools to further customize their web experience.

Mobile (Android and iOS)

  • Both Android and iOS operating systems offer a wide range of built-in accessibility tools, including:
    • Screen readers (e.g., VoiceOver on iOS, TalkBack on Android)
    • Magnification and zoom features
    • Adjustable font sizes and contrast settings
    • Closed captioning and subtitles for videos
    • Voice control and dictation
  • We encourage users to explore the accessibility settings on their devices to customize their experience.

KindredCircl's Commitment to Accessibility

We are continually working to improve the accessibility of our platform and to ensure compatibility with commonly used accessibility tools. We strive to adhere to accessibility guidelines and best practices in our design and development processes. We are working to ensure that all images will have alt text.

User Feedback

We welcome feedback from users regarding accessibility issues or suggestions for improvement. Please contact us at accessibility@kindredcircl.com to report any accessibility challenges or to provide feedback. We will provide instructions on how to access the accessibility features of KindredCircl within the application help section

NSFW (Not Safe For Work) Content:

Refer to the Definitions section for the definition of NSFW

General Guidelines:

Content that is gratuitously violent, or exploits, abuses, or endangers children is strictly prohibited.

Content that promotes illegal sexual acts, such as rape, bestiality, or child sexual abuse, is strictly prohibited.

Acceptable Nudity:

Nudity that is artistic, educational, or related to personal expression (e.g., body positivity, nudist communities) is permitted, provided it complies with other community guidelines and is marked NSFW.

Context is important. Nudity that is presented in a sexual or exploitative manner will be removed.

Sexual Discussions:

Open and respectful discussions about sexuality, including topics such as puberty, sexual health, and relationships, are permitted.

Adult-only Circls marked as NSFW may engage in more explicit discussions and share certain forms of legal pornography, provided they adhere to the following:

  • Content must be legal and not promote illegal acts.
  • Content must be consensual and not depict non-consensual acts.
  • The Circl's purpose must be clearly stated, and membership must be restricted to adults.
  • Users attempting to join NSFW Circls will be required to verify their age through a reputable third-party age verification service. This process ensures that only adults can access explicit content.
  • NSFW Circls may include depictions of consensual nudity and sexual activity, provided they do not depict illegal or non-consensual acts, exploitation, or abuse. Content that promotes violence, hate speech, or illegal activities is strictly prohibited, even within NSFW Circls.
  • Circl Owners and Leaders of these Circls are responsible for ensuring that all content complies with these guidelines and for promptly addressing any violations. Failure to do so may result in the removal of moderator privileges or the closure of the Circl.
  • To report violations within an NSFW Circl, please use the standard reporting mechanisms or contact the Circl Owner or Leader directly. All reports will be handled confidentially.
  • The context and intent of any content will be carefully considered. Nudity or sexual content that is presented in an exploitative, degrading, or non-consensual manner will be removed, regardless of the Circl's designation
  • Links to external websites containing NSFW content are permitted within NSFW Circls, provided the content complies with these guidelines. However, linking to illegal or harmful content is strictly prohibited.

Circl Owners and Leaders

Circls are managed by volunteer Circl Owners and Leaders, who are members of the KindredCircl community.

Circl Owners and Leaders are responsible for:

  • Setting and enforcing Circl-specific guidelines, within the bounds of the KindredCircl Community Guidelines.
  • Fostering a positive and respectful environment within their Circl.
  • Moderating discussions and content to ensure compliance with KindredCircl Community Guidelines.
  • Addressing user concerns and resolving disputes within their Circl.
  • Reporting serious violations of the KindredCircl Community Guidelines to KindredCircl moderators.

Circl Owners and Leaders are granted certain administrative privileges within their Circl, but they are not employees of KindredCircl.

KindredCircl moderators may provide guidance and support to Circl Owners and Leaders, but they do not manage individual Circls.

KindredCircl reserves the right to remove Circl Owners or Leaders who violate the Community Guidelines or abuse their administrative privileges.

Moderation

Enforcement of Guidelines:

KindredCircl moderators, who are company employees, will enforce these guidelines to ensure a safe, inclusive, and positive community environment.

Moderators will proactively monitor content and respond to user reports.

AI-Assisted Moderation:

KindredCircl employs AI-powered tools to proactively identify potentially violating content. Users can also flag content for review by moderators. AI and user flags trigger a review process by our moderation team.

Content Review Process:

  1. When content is flagged for review, it will be assessed by our moderation team. Depending on the nature of the alleged violation, the content may be deprioritized (reduced visibility) or removed while the review is pending.
  2. Deprioritization: Content flagged for potential spam, misinformation, or copyright violations will be deprioritized during the review process. This means that its visibility may be reduced but it will remain accessible.
  3. Removal: Content flagged for illegal activity, harassment/bullying, or CSAM will be immediately removed pending review. This ensures the safety of our community
  4. All user reports will be investigated by a moderator.

Impartial and Fair Decisions:

Moderators will act impartially and fairly in all decisions, applying the guidelines consistently.

Moderators will receive comprehensive internal training to ensure they understand and apply the guidelines correctly.

Moderators will document their decisions and the rationale behind them.

Moderator Training and Confidentiality:

Moderators will undergo rigorous initial and ongoing training to equip them to handle various moderation scenarios.

To protect the safety and privacy of our moderation team, specific details of moderator training programs will remain confidential.

The identities of individual moderators will be protected to prevent harassment and ensure their safety.

Transparency and Accountability:

Moderation actions will be documented, and users will be provided with clear explanations for actions taken on their content or accounts.

Moderators will be held accountable for their actions through internal review processes.

Community Collaboration:

KindredCircl will work with the community to identify and address emerging moderation challenges, while protecting the privacy of the moderation team.

KindredCircl will provide channels for users to provide feedback on moderation practices, without requiring direct contact with individual moderators.

Escalation Procedures:

There will be clear internal escalation procedures for moderators to follow when dealing with difficult situations.

A team of senior moderators or administrators will be available for internal support and escalation.

Reporting:

If you encounter content that you believe violates these guidelines, please report it to the community moderators.

Provide clear and specific details about the content and why you believe it is in violation.

False Reports:

  • KindredCircl takes all reports of violations seriously. However, making false reports is a serious offense that can have negative consequences for the reported user and undermine the effectiveness of our reporting system.
  • Definition: KA false report is any report that is deliberately fabricated, misleading, or made without a genuine belief that a violation of the Community Guidelines has occurred.
  • Prohibited Conduct: Users are strictly prohibited from making false reports.
  • Consequences: Making false reports may result in:
    • A warning from KindredCircl moderators.
    • Temporary suspension of the user's account.
    • Permanent termination of the user's account.
    • In cases where false reports lead to legal action, KindredCircl may cooperate with law enforcement.
  • Reporting False Reports: If you believe you have been the target of a false report, you may submit a counter-report to [false report email address]. Please provide evidence to support your claim.
  • Good Faith Reporting: We encourage users to report any content or behavior that they genuinely believe violates our Community Guidelines. However, we also expect users to exercise good judgment and avoid making frivolous or malicious reports.

Dispute Resolution:

KindredCircl encourages respectful communication and cooperation among its users. However, we understand that disputes may arise from time to time.

Types of Disputes:

KindredCircl moderators will primarily address disputes related to violations of the Community Guidelines.

Disputes between users regarding personal matters, business transactions, or other external issues are generally outside the scope of KindredCircl's involvement.

Disputes that involve legal obligations, and data privacy will be viewed according to the Terms of Service, and Privacy Policy respectively.

Encouraging Amicable Resolution:

We encourage users to attempt to resolve disputes directly and respectfullyo We encourage users to attempt to resolve disputes directly and respectfully.

If direct communication fails, users may seek assistance from Circl Owners or Leaders, where applicable.

Moderation Involvement:

KindredCircl moderators may intervene in disputes that involve clear violations of the Community Guidelines.

Moderators will strive to remain impartial and make decisions based on the available evidence and the guidelines.

Escalation:

If a user is dissatisfied with a moderator's decision, they may follow the appeals process outlined in the "Appeals Process" section.

Circl-specific disputes may be escalated to KindredCircl moderators if they involve serious violations of the Community Guidelines, Terms of Service, or Privacy Policy..

Limitations:

KindredCircl is not a legal or judicial body and cannot provide legal advice or enforce legal agreements.

KindredCircl's involvement in disputes is limited to enforcing the Community Guidelines, Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and maintaining a safe and respectful environment.

KindredCircl is not responsible for any damages or losses resulting from user disputes.

KindredCircl policies that may pertain to disputes include, but are not limited to, the Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and DMCA policy.

Appeals Process:

If your content is removed or flagged for violating these guidelines, you may appeal the decision by contacting the community moderators.

Provide a clear explanation of why you believe the content is compliant with the guidelines.

Provide a clear explanation of why you believe the content is compliant with the guidelines.

Tier 1 Appeal:

  • Initial appeals will be reviewed by the original moderator or a different moderator from the same team.
  • Tier 1 appeals will be reviewed and responded to within 48 hours of submission.

Tier 2 Appeal:

  • If the Tier 1 appeal is denied, you may request a second-level appeal.
  • You have 72 hours from the notification of the denial of your Tier 1 appeal to submit your Tier 2 appeal.
  • Tier 2 appeals will be reviewed by a senior moderator or a designated appeals team.
  • Tier 2 appeals will be completed and the results delivered within one week of submission.

All time limits are calculated in calendar days, including weekends and holidays.

Users will receive confirmation of receipt for all appeals.

Users will be notified if there are unexpected delays.

The decision of the Tier 2 appeal is final.

Definitions

A

Account Suspension: The temporary disabling of a user's account on KindredCircl.

Account Termination: The permanent removal of a user's account from KindredCircl.

Astroturfing: The practice of masking the sponsors of a message or organization to give the appearance of grassroots support.

B

Beacon: A public post shared by a user on KindredCircl, visible to other users.

Block: To prevent a user from interacting with you on KindredCircl, including viewing your profile, Beacons, Reflections, and sending Whispers.

Brigading: A coordinated online attack, typically involving a large group of people, aimed at overwhelming or silencing a target.

Bullying: A form of harassment that involves repeated and persistent negative behavior.

C

Circls: A designated area within KindredCircl where users with shared interests can interact.

Comment: A user's written response to a Beacon or Reflection, or another user's comment.

Content: Any text, images, videos, audio, or other materials posted or shared on KindredCircl.

Copyright Infringement: The use of copyrighted material without permission from the copyright owner.

CSAM (Child Sexual Abuse Material): Any visual depiction of sexually explicit conduct involving a minor, including real, computer-generated, or drawn content.

D

Deepfake: A manipulated video or audio recording that appears to be genuine.

Disinformation: False or inaccurate information that is spread intentionally to deceive.

Diverse Opinions: Viewpoints on debatable topics where reasonable people can disagree. These are often areas of social, political, economic, or philosophical discussion. Claims that contradict established scientific, historical, or factual consensus, or that rely on misinformation, are not considered diverse opinions. KindredCircl encourages evidence-based arguments and critical thinking in all discussions.

DM (Direct Message): A private message sent directly from one user to one or more other users.

Doxing: The act of publicly revealing private or identifying information about an individual or organization, usually with malicious intent.

F

Fair Use: A legal doctrine that allows limited use of copyrighted material without permission for purposes such as criticism, commentary, news reporting, teaching, scholarship, or research.

H

Harassment: Any unwelcome conduct that is intended to intimidate, offend, or humiliate another person, including cyberstalking, doxing, and sharing private information without consent.

Hate Speech: Any content or behavior that promotes hatred, discrimination, or violence based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, gender identity, gender expression, sexual orientation, disability, age, national origin, veteran status, socioeconomic status, or any other protected characteristic.

I

Impersonation: The act of pretending to be another person.

Intellectual Property: Creations of the mind, such as inventions, literary and artistic works, designs, and symbols, names, and images used in commerce.

K

Kin: A user's established connections on KindredCircl, representing close relationships.

M

Malicious Impersonation: Impersonation with the intent to deceive, defraud, or harm.

Misinformation: False or inaccurate information that is spread unintentionally.

Moderator: A KindredCircl employee responsible for enforcing the community guidelines.

Mute: To hide a user's Beacons, Reflections, and comments from your view without blocking them.

N

Notifications: Alerts sent to users regarding activity on KindredCircl, such as new Beacons, Reflections, Whispers, or comments.

NSFW (Not Safe for Work): Content that is considered inappropriate for viewing in a professional or public setting.

P

Private Information: Information that can be used to identify an individual, such as their full name, address, phone number, or social security number.

Profile: A user's personal space on KindredCircl, containing information they choose to share, and their Beacons and Reflections.

R

Reflection: A private post shared by a user on KindredCircl, representing personal thoughts or journal entries, visible only to the user or selected Kin.

Report or Reporting: The action of notifying KindredCircl moderators about content that violates these Community Guidelines.

S

Search: The function that allows users to find content, Circls, or users on KindredCircl.

T

Terms of Service: The legal agreement between KindredCircl and its users, outlining the rules and conditions of using the platform.

Tier 1 Appeal: The first step in the appeals process, reviewed by the original moderator or a different moderator from the same team.

Tier 2 Appeal: The second step in the appeals process, reviewed by a senior moderator or a designated appeals team.

U

User or Member: Any person that has created an account, and is using the KindredCircl platform.

W

Whispers: A private direct message sent directly from one user to one or more other users.
